# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to the OctoAcme project management documentation hub! This README serves as your central entry point for understanding how OctoAcme runs projects, manages teams, and delivers value to customers.

## Overview

OctoAcme follows a **customer-first, iterative delivery** approach to project management. Our process emphasizes:

- **Customer-centric development**: Prioritizing customer value and usability in every decision
- **Iterative delivery**: Shipping small, testable increments to enable rapid feedback and course correction
- **Clear ownership**: Each project has a named Project Manager (PM) and Product Lead ensuring accountability
- **Data-informed decisions**: Measuring impact and iterating based on evidence rather than assumptions
- **Psychological safety**: Creating an environment where team members feel empowered to provide feedback and learn from mistakes

## Key Roles & Personas

OctoAcme projects are driven by three core roles working in close collaboration:

- **Project Manager (PM)**: Coordinates delivery activities, manages schedules and risks, facilitates team communication, and ensures transparency across stakeholders
- **Product Manager (PdM)**: Defines product outcomes, prioritizes the backlog, sets success metrics, and validates solutions through user research
- **Developers**: Implement features, collaborate on design decisions, write tests, and help identify technical risks
- **QA/Testing**: Validate quality standards, verify acceptance criteria, and ensure features meet requirements
- **Stakeholders**: Provide strategic inputs, approvals, and business context

## Project Lifecycle

OctoAcme projects follow a structured lifecycle with five key phases:

1. **Initiation**: Define the problem statement, identify stakeholders, establish success metrics, and create a high-level timeline
2. **Planning**: Break work into shippable increments, identify dependencies and risks, and align on timelines and responsibilities
3. **Execution**: Build, test, review, and iterate on features following defined workflows and quality standards
4. **Release & Deployment**: Deploy to production with appropriate verification, rollback plans, and stakeholder communication
5. **Retrospective**: Capture learnings, celebrate successes, and convert insights into actionable improvements

## Communication & Collaboration

Effective communication is critical to OctoAcme's success. Our standard communication cadence includes:

- **Daily standups** (15 min): Focus on progress, blockers, and dependencies
- **Weekly PM + PdM sync**: Alignment on priorities, risks, and strategic decisions
- **Twice-weekly delivery team standups**: Coordination and planning (or as agreed by the team)
- **Sprint/milestone demos**: Showcase progress and gather feedback
- **Monthly stakeholder updates**: High-level status and upcoming priorities
- **Ad-hoc escalations**: Handled through defined escalation paths as needed

## Quality Assurance Practices

OctoAcme maintains high quality standards through:

- **Automated testing**: Unit tests, integration tests, and end-to-end smoke tests for critical flows
- **Pull request workflow**: Small PRs with clear acceptance criteria, automated CI checks, and peer review before merging
- **Security scanning**: Integrated into CI pipeline to catch vulnerabilities early
- **Definition of Done**: Clear, documented criteria that all work must meet before being considered complete
- **Risk management**: Proactive identification, assessment, and mitigation of project risks through a maintained Risk Register
